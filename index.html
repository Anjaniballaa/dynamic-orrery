About the Project

What is
Interstellar?

Interstellar is a dynamic web application that lets you explore the wonders of our solar system in immersive 3D — powered by Three.js and real NASA data.

Navigate through planets, asteroids, and comets. Gain insights into their orbital characteristics and behaviors. Test your knowledge with interactive exams.

Built by computer science students of SVECW as part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge.

Getting Started

01
Explore 3D

Click Explore Now to enter the 3D solar system. Hover over the Sun and planets to view live information about each body.

02
Select Planet

Use the Celestial dropdown to jump to any planet's detail page — orbital data, NASA imagery, and more.

03
Take the Exam

Test your knowledge of the solar system with our interactive quiz. Click Start Exam on any planet page.
